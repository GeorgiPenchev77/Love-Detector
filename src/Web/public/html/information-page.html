About Love Detector:

The Love Detector is a speed dating tool where users can discover the potential of a deeper connection while engaging in fun conversation. We start with individually measure the heartbeats of both users to establish baseline for comparison for the final match.
The measurement is done with sensors that are attached to the users earlobes. Then we proceed on measuring the users heartbeats during a ‘date’ where they engage in conversation based on displayed questions.
Throughout the date new measuring data is collected. By the end of the ‘date’ the data is analysed based on the variations and ‘spikes’.
The final match is determined by comparing this data between users, resulting in one of three level: no match, decent match, or absolute match.
